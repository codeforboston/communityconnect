Suggesting Health Resources in Revere, Chelsea, Charlestown, and Boston

Hello! Community Connect is a web application that aims to consolidate information about businesses and organization available in communities that promote healthy lifestyle choices. With the support of Revere CARES Coalition and Massachusetts General Hospital, we are looking to compile an extensive list of businesses and organizations in the Revere, Charlestown, and Boston area that can help its citizens live a healthy and happy life. If you live in the area or know of resources available in the area, feel free to suggest resources that are not currently listed on the web application.

Please note: Suggested health resources may or may not be accepted by professionals from MGH and Revere CARES.
